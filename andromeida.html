    About Us




    Expanding from conceptualising some of the most advanced underwater robots in Indian subcontinent to
    launching a dynamic learning vertical, Androlearn, Andromeida’s development streak has been multi-
    faceted.

    We strive to achieve excellence in capturing, analysing and planning mitigation strategies for research
    operations in any given underwater environment.

    By the virtue of transformation, advancements in Artificial intelligence have poised to build empowering
    trust and we are the firm believers of engineering for the ocean to set affirmative foundations for future of the mankind.

    In accordance to the Blue Economy Policy by United Nations, we’ve commenced our development towards preservation and restoration of recreational water(s). Pool Buddy is our flagship offering to restore human belief in the quality of pools they use. Celebrated for its performance and endurance across the northern sub-region, this robot sets apart a unique SoP to balance any water back to its optimum quality by restoring its chemistry.

    We have evolved as the most preferred choice of communities as our passion for research to implement RoS based systems, beginning from Ground ‘0’ spearheads the mission to normalise robots and underwater technology to comfort human muscles.
